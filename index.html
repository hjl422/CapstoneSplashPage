Introducing COCINA

When learning a new language, engagement and immersion are difficult to achieve without travel. COCINA, a Google Home assistant, seeks to solve this problem by allowing language learners to practice conversations in the comfort of their own kitchen. By providing recipe guidance in Spanish and a translated version in English, our users will be able to cook a cultural dish while practicing their listening, comprehension, and conversational skills in Spanish.

It's as simple as 1, 2, 3

  • Recipe

    Let COCINA know what you'd like to cook, or choose from our recipes list.

  • Ingredients

    Listen to the ingredients list, prepare your supplies, and get ready!

  • Cook

    Follow along with each step - at any time, you can pause, repeat, and ask for help.

Our Research

While conducting our research, we found that smart learning environments offer rich opportunities for language learners.[1] Language learner have a much better experience having to use their language skills and do an activity, rather than merely getting corrected in reading and writing.

  • 85% Users indicated lack of practice
  • 90% Users want a language exchange buddy
  • 100% Users indicated the lack of resources to find language buddies
  • 80% Users like cooking
  • 90% Users fell in love with our product!
