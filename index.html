Introducing COCINA

Cocina is a Google Home Assistant that allows users to learn and practice a language by cooking recipes in that language.

It's as simple as 1, 2, 3

  • Recipe

    Let COCINA know what you'd like to cook, or choose from our recipes list.

  • Ingredients

    Listen to the ingredients list, prepare your supplies, and get ready!

  • Cook

    Follow along with each step - at any time, you can pause, repeat, and ask for help.

  • Try it now in our demo system!

Our Research

We found that smart learning environments offer rich opportunities for language learners.[1]

  • 85% Users indicated current lack of practice with a second language
  • 90% Users want a language exchange buddy
  • 100% Users indicated the lack of resources to find language buddies
  • 80% Users like cooking and/or do it frequently at home
  • 90% Users fell in love with our product!

We sought to create an experience that would be authentic, educational, and fun! We chose the Google Home VUI for its portability and ease of access, where having a conversation would seamlessly blend in with a user's daily routine.

The iSchool capstone

Capstone projects are the culmination of students' learning experience at the iSchool. Students distill the knowledge and skills acquired in academic courses and apply them to a real-world project, developing the approach, conducting the research, and presenting their findings in both oral and written formats as well as the final project solution.
